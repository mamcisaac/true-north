How to play

The game names a real place somewhere on Earth. Hold your finger on the compass, physically point the top of your phone toward where you think it is, then release to lock your guess.

The compass then reveals the true direction (measured from your location along the great-circle path) and how many degrees off you were. Five places per game — the lower your average error, the better.

On a computer (no compass), drag the needle around the dial to aim instead.
